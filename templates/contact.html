A little about the author:

Kat is 20 years old and studies computer science and exercise science at Ithaca College. Kat has been drawn to electronic/EDM type music since she was just in elementary school. She notes that Mr. Kitty opened the door for her to explore new artists with similar sounds. Kat is a huge fan of Mr. Kitty and hopes to one day see him live. Kat listens to Mr. Kitty so frequently, that a website specific to their music seemed like a great idea. Kat hopes that this website will be a fun way for fans to engage with each other and share their favorite songs.
